Word Garden

*＊✿❀○❀✿＊*

Use this website as your garden to plant thoughts, dreams, feelings, secrets, poems, dialogues, or whatever.

  • ＊ Type in what you want to plant.
  • ＊ Organize by dragging plants around.
  • ＊ Decorate by changing colors of the field and plants.
  • ＊ View in full screen by pressing Play button.

*＊✿❀○❀✿＊*

Word Garden is designed and created by Anh Ngo, who finds collaging unfinished sentences and separate words a meditative form of journaling.
